#Mathematics 
#LieTheory

BCH is the solution to the formula:
$$
e^X e^Y=e^Z
$$
for possibly non-commutative $X$ and $Y$ in the [[Lie Algebra]] of a [[Lie Group]].
$$
Z=X+Y+\frac{1}{2}[X, Y]+\frac{1}{12}[X,[X, Y]]-\frac{1}{12}[Y,[X, Y]]+\cdots,
$$
where the commutator of $X$ and $Y$ is:
$$
[X, Y] = XY - YX
$$
